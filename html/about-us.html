Our Story (私たちの物語)

Sushi Bite was founded in 2015 in the heart of Tokyo, where tradition meets innovation. What began as a humble sushi-ya in Shinjuku has blossomed into a beloved culinary destination, known for its dedication to authentic Edo-style sushi with a modern twist. Our chefs, trained under master sushi artisans, craft each piece with precision, using only the freshest seafood sourced from Tsukiji Market and local suppliers. At Sushi Bite, we honor Japan’s rich culinary heritage while embracing contemporary flavors, creating a dining experience that resonates with both locals and visitors.

Our Vision (ビジョン)

To be the most trusted and innovative sushi restaurant in Japan, preserving tradition while pioneering the future of Japanese cuisine.

Our Mission (ビジョン)

  • Serve only the finest, seasonal ingredients with masterful technique.
  • Deliver heartfelt service that reflects true Japanese warmth.
  • Blend tradition with creativity through new sushi styles and digital convenience.
  • Support local fisheries and eco-friendly practices to protect Japan’s culinary future.
